Are you tired of using a standard mouse for your daily computing tasks? Are you too hip to use a mouse in public but secretly pine for the basic amenities of the 1988 GUI? Magneto-mouse is simple, efficient and more hip than a typewriter in Starbucks. Slip a small magnetic ring on your index finger and place the sensor above your keyboard. Type as you normally would and when you need to move the cursor just point at the screen and move it!

Do you need another paragraph of convincing? How about a picture of how painful using a regular 'ol mouse is. Look at the anguish in those hands. It's just as painful as watching Goose eject from that fighter jet, knowing he would never make it back to earth alive.
